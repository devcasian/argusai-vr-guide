Argus AI Pro Tutorial

Learn the gestures and features of the game.

Move Objects with Pinch Gesture

Use the Pinch gesture to grab an object and move it to your desired position. Simply pinch with your hand to pick up the object, then drag it to the desired location.

Resize Objects

To resize an object, use the Pinch gesture with both hands. Grab the object with one hand and again with the other, then pull your hands apart to enlarge it or bring them closer together to shrink it.

Open and Close the Settings Menu

Use the Thumbs Up gesture with your right hand to open or close the settings menu. Position your right hand in front of you and give a thumbs-up gesture to activate the menu.

Change Render Mode

Use the Left Thumb gesture to switch between render modes. There are three options available: Direct Volume Rendering, Maximum Intensity Projection, and Isosurface Rendering.

Cross Section Feature

Enable the Cross Section mode to create slices of the volumetric image. Place the object under the cross-section plane, then rotate it to achieve the desired slice orientation.

Menu Settings

  • Min Value and Max Value: Adjust sliders to change intensity and density.
  • Position Reset: Resets the object's position to appear in front of the player's eyes.
  • Size Reset: Restores the object's size to its original dimensions.
  • Cross: Activates or deactivates the cross-section plane.
  • Mode: Changes the render mode through the menu.
